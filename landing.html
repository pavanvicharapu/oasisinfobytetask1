COVID-19 Safety Guidelines

Protect Yourself and Others

Overview

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. It spreads primarily through respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or talks. To prevent the spread of COVID-19, follow these safety guidelines:

Safety Measures

1. Wear a mask in public places.

2. Practice physical distancing (stay at least 6 feet apart).

3. Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

4. Avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose, and mouth.

5. Stay home if you're feeling unwell or have COVID-19 symptoms.
